About Me

Welcome to my personal website! I am a doctoral student pursuing a PhD in the area of sodium ion batteries at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Bhopal under the guidance of Professor Rohit Ranganathan.

  • Name: Yashraj Soni
  • Age: 24
  • Occupation: Doctoral Student

My research interests lie in developing low-cost and efficient energy storage devices for renewable energy systems, and sodium ion batteries are a promising alternative to traditional lithium-ion batteries due to the abundance and low cost of sodium resources. In my research, I explore the fundamental principles of sodium ion battery operation and design innovative electrode materials for enhancing their electrochemical performance. At IISER Bhopal, I have access to state-of-the-art research facilities and a vibrant academic community, which has helped me to broaden my knowledge and skills in the field. I have also collaborated with researchers from different disciplines to explore the interdisciplinary aspects of energy storage systems. Apart from my research, I am also passionate about science outreach and communication. Through my personal website, I aim to share my research findings, insights, and experiences with a broader audience, including fellow researchers, students, and the general public. I also hope to inspire the next generation of scientists and encourage more young people to pursue research in the field of energy storage and renewable energy. Thank you for visiting my website, and feel free to reach out to me if you have any questions or feedback.
